Sunday Care Schedule · operated by xlSigma, LLC · Last updated: May 31, 2026

Program name and description

Sunday Care Schedule Reminders. This program sends SMS text reminders to family caregivers about their assigned Sunday eldercare shift (typically 6:00–8:00 PM), including the date and a link to the private scheduling app.

Message frequency

Message frequency is recurring but low — approximately one message per week to a recipient on the weeks they are scheduled, sent only when the family organizer sends a reminder. Frequency varies.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier's standard messaging and data charges apply to messages you send or receive.

Opt-out and help

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts. For help, reply HELP or contact the organizer at andresslack@hotmail.com.

Supported carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Service availability may vary by carrier.

Privacy

Your information is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We do not share mobile information with third parties for marketing purposes.
